Travel Planning Checklist

Field design, operating steps, and a downloadable starter template to confirm bookings, transport, connectivity, insurance, documents, and emergency contacts.

Purpose of this template

The Travel Planning Checklist helps teams confirm bookings, transport, connectivity, insurance, documents, and emergency contacts. Before adding fields, decide who updates each item, when it is updated, and what evidence supports it.

Download the Markdown template

Decisions to make first

  • what one row or checklist item represents
  • who enters and who reviews information
  • update frequency and deadline
  • completion, approval, and retention rules

How to start

  1. Download or copy the template.
  2. Remove fields that do not support your process.
  3. Test calculations and workflow with sample data.
  4. Run it for a week or month and remove fields that are not maintained.

Operational cautions

A template does not replace documents required by law or contract. Review applicable rules for personal data, contracts, tax, employment, and retention.

Key point

More fields do not automatically produce better information. Keep only fields used in a decision.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use it without changes?

Test it with sample data and remove fields that do not match your owners, deadlines, and approval flow.

Can I use it in Google Sheets?

CSV files can be imported, while Markdown checklists can be pasted into Docs, Notion, or similar tools.

Can it contain personal data?

Review access, retention, and sharing. Do not collect personal information that is unnecessary for the purpose.
