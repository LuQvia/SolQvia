Noboribetsu Onsen and Jigokudani Guide

Plan Jigokudani, walking paths, day bathing, and an overnight stay in Noboribetsu Onsen.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide

  2. Main highlights

Why visit

Noboribetsu is a major Hokkaido onsen area shaped by volcanic terrain and varied spring waters. Treat the Jigokudani walk and bathing as separate experiences, adjusting for sulfur, snow, closures, and health.

Keep the route focused and confirm the return connection, weather alternative, and primary purpose before departure so transport does not consume the destination time.

Main highlights

  • Jigokudani volcanic scenery
  • Walking paths toward Oyunuma
  • Public or day-use bathing in the onsen town
  • Multiple spring types at accommodation

Suggested order

  1. Begin with Jigokudani volcanic scenery as the main objective.
  2. Continue in the same direction to Walking paths toward Oyunuma.
  3. If time and energy remain, add Public or day-use bathing in the onsen town or Multiple spring types at accommodation.
  4. Preserve buffer time for meals, rest, luggage, and the return journey.

Access

Use a local bus from JR Noboribetsu Station to the onsen town. Check reservations for direct airport or Sapporo buses and hotel transfers.

Check before visiting

  • Check current trail access
  • Avoid prolonged bathing according to health and water properties
  • Prepare for icy surfaces in winter
  • Do not approach wildlife
Key point

Hours, prices, transport, entry rules, construction, natural conditions, and seasonal events change. Recheck official facility, local-government, and transport sources shortly before departure.

Frequently asked questions

How much time should I allow?

Allow half a day for walking and a day bath, or stay overnight for several baths and dinner.

Can I visit by public transport?

Use a local bus from JR Noboribetsu Station to the onsen town. Check reservations for direct airport or Sapporo buses and hotel transfers.

What should I verify before departure?

Check current hours, transport schedules, weather, and entry restrictions. For mountains, islands, coasts, and onsen areas, also review closures, cancellations, and natural conditions.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
