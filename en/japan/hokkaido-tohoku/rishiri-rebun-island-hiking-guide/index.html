Rishiri and Rebun Island Hiking Guide

Plan flowers, coastlines, hiking, and ferry travel across Rishiri and Rebun islands.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Why visit
  2. Main highlights
  3. Suggested order
  4. Access
  5. Check before visiting

Why visit

Rishiri and Rebun are remote northern islands known for Mount Rishiri, coastal scenery, and alpine flowers. Ferry cancellations and weather changes make buffer time essential.

Keep the route focused and confirm the return connection, weather alternative, and primary purpose before departure so transport does not consume the destination time.

Main highlights

  • Rebun flower and coastal trails
  • Scenic travel around Rishiri
  • Mount Rishiri hiking or viewpoints
  • Island food including kelp and seafood

Suggested order

  1. Begin with Rebun flower and coastal trails as the main objective.
  2. Continue in the same direction to Scenic travel around Rishiri.
  3. If time and energy remain, add Mount Rishiri hiking or viewpoints or Island food including kelp and seafood.
  4. Preserve buffer time for meals, rest, luggage, and the return journey.

Access

Take ferries from Wakkanai. Island buses, rental cars, and bicycles have limited schedules and supply, so arrange them in advance.

Check before visiting

  • Check ferries, waves, and wind
  • Prepare registration, gear, and water for hiking
  • Do not step on or collect rare plants
  • Keep a buffer day for disrupted transport
Key point

Hours, prices, transport, entry rules, construction, natural conditions, and seasonal events change. Recheck official facility, local-government, and transport sources shortly before departure.

Frequently asked questions

How much time should I allow?

Stay at least one night for one island or three nights or more for both islands.

Can I visit by public transport?

Take ferries from Wakkanai. Island buses, rental cars, and bicycles have limited schedules and supply, so arrange them in advance.

What should I verify before departure?

Check current hours, transport schedules, weather, and entry restrictions. For mountains, islands, coasts, and onsen areas, also review closures, cancellations, and natural conditions.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
