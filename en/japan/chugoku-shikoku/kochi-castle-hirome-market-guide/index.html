Kochi Castle and Hirome Market Guide

Plan Kochi Castle, Sunday Market, Hirome Market, and riverside areas by tram and walking.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Why visit
  2. Main highlights
  3. Suggested order
  4. Access

Why visit

Central Kochi places its original castle keep, shopping streets, and markets within walking distance. Visit the castle first and shift meal time away from peak crowds.

Keep the route focused and confirm the return connection, weather alternative, and primary purpose before departure so transport does not consume the destination time.

Main highlights

  • Kochi Castle keep and honmaru palace
  • Sunday Market on Otesuji
  • Hirome Market and bonito cuisine
  • Harimayabashi and Kagami River area

Suggested order

  1. Begin with Kochi Castle keep and honmaru palace as the main objective.
  2. Continue in the same direction to Sunday Market on Otesuji.
  3. If time and energy remain, add Hirome Market and bonito cuisine or Harimayabashi and Kagami River area.
  4. Preserve buffer time for meals, rest, luggage, and the return journey.

Access

Use trams, local buses, and walking from Kochi Station. Review road and transport changes during the Sunday Market.

Check before visiting

  • Prepare for steep castle stairs
  • Check Sunday Market date and hours
  • Use shared seating considerately and manage alcohol
  • Prepare for strong summer sun
Key point

Hours, prices, transport, entry rules, construction, natural conditions, and seasonal events change. Recheck official facility, local-government, and transport sources shortly before departure.

Frequently asked questions

How much time should I allow?

Allow half a day for castle and market or a full day with Sunday Market, museums, and the river.

Can I visit by public transport?

Use trams, local buses, and walking from Kochi Station. Review road and transport changes during the Sunday Market.

What should I verify before departure?

Check current hours, transport schedules, weather, and entry restrictions. For mountains, islands, coasts, and onsen areas, also review closures, cancellations, and natural conditions.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
