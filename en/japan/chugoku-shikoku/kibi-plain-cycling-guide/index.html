Kibi Plain Cycling Guide

Cycle among Kibi tombs, temples, and farmland using rental bicycles and rail for a one-way route.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Why visit
  2. Main highlights
  3. Suggested order
  4. Access
  5. Check before visiting

Why visit

The Kibi Plain spreads burial mounds, temples, and farmland between Okayama and Soja. A one-way station-to-station route is efficient, but rental and return points require advance confirmation.

Keep the route focused and confirm the return connection, weather alternative, and primary purpose before departure so transport does not consume the destination time.

Main highlights

  • Kibitsu Shrine and approach
  • Burial mounds including Tsukuriyama
  • Bitchu Kokubunji five-story pagoda
  • Cycling through fields and irrigation channels

Suggested order

  1. Begin with Kibitsu Shrine and approach as the main objective.
  2. Continue in the same direction to Burial mounds including Tsukuriyama.
  3. If time and energy remain, add Bitchu Kokubunji five-story pagoda or Cycling through fields and irrigation channels.
  4. Preserve buffer time for meals, rest, luggage, and the return journey.

Access

Use JR Kibi or Hakubi Line stations as start and finish points with a rental bicycle. Confirm return arrangements and train times first.

Check before visiting

  • Wear a helmet and follow traffic rules
  • Check rental hours and return points
  • Prepare for heat and sun in summer
  • Do not enter tombs, farmland, or private property without permission
Key point

Hours, prices, transport, entry rules, construction, natural conditions, and seasonal events change. Recheck official facility, local-government, and transport sources shortly before departure.

Frequently asked questions

How much time should I allow?

Allow half a day for selected stops or a full day for the principal Kibi route.

Can I visit by public transport?

Use JR Kibi or Hakubi Line stations as start and finish points with a rental bicycle. Confirm return arrangements and train times first.

What should I verify before departure?

Check current hours, transport schedules, weather, and entry restrictions. For mountains, islands, coasts, and onsen areas, also review closures, cancellations, and natural conditions.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
