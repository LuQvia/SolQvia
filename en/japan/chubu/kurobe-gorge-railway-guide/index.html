Kurobe Gorge Railway Guide

Plan the trolley train from Unazuki and short gorge walks around current operations.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Why visit
  2. Main highlights
  3. Suggested order

Why visit

The Kurobe Gorge Railway is a seasonal sightseeing line through a deep gorge. Operating sections and recovery work can change, so verify the current reachable area before booking.

Keep the route focused and confirm the return connection first so that transport does not consume the time intended for the destination.

Main highlights

  • The trolley train from Unazuki
  • Kurobe River and steep gorge scenery
  • Walking paths around intermediate stations
  • Seasonal mountain scenery including autumn foliage

Suggested order

  1. Begin with The trolley train from Unazuki.
  2. Continue in the same direction to Kurobe River and steep gorge scenery.
  3. If time remains, add Walking paths around intermediate stations or Seasonal mountain scenery including autumn foliage.
  4. Preserve buffer time for food, rest, and the return journey.

Access

Take Toyama Chihou Railway to Unazuki Onsen and walk to the gorge railway station. Confirm connections in both directions.

Check before visiting

  • Check the current operating section and reservation rules
  • Bring warm clothing for open-sided cars
  • Review walking-path closures
  • Secure the return train first
Key point

Hours, prices, transport, entry rules, construction, and seasonal events change. Recheck official facility, local-government, and transport sources shortly before departure.

Frequently asked questions

How much time should I allow?

Allow half to a full day for the return ride and short walks, or stay overnight in Unazuki Onsen.

Can I visit by public transport?

Take Toyama Chihou Railway to Unazuki Onsen and walk to the gorge railway station. Confirm connections in both directions.

What should I verify before departure?

Check current hours, transport schedules, weather, and entry restrictions. For mountain, coastal, and onsen areas, also review service suspensions and road closures.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
