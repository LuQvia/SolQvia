Yufuin Onsen and Lake Kinrin Guide

Walk from Yufuin Station through the shopping streets to Lake Kinrin and onsen accommodation.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Who this destination suits
  2. Main highlights
  3. Access and suggested approach

Who this destination suits

Yufuin is an onsen town beneath Mount Yufu, with shops and small museums leading from the station toward Lake Kinrin. Day trips focus on walking, while an overnight stay offers onsen and quieter mornings and evenings.

For a first visit, connect the main sights in one logical direction and reserve transport and rest time before adding optional stops.

Main highlights

  • Town views beneath Mount Yufu
  • Yunotsubo shopping street and small museums
  • Walks around Lake Kinrin
  • Onsen accommodation and rural scenery

Access and suggested approach

Reach Yufuin Station by rail or bus from Hakata, Oita, or Beppu. The center is explored on foot or by bicycle.

  1. Confirm the arrival point and return transport first.
  2. Choose one non-negotiable highlight.
  3. Connect two or three nearby stops in the same direction.
  4. Leave buffer time for food, rest, and delays.

Check before visiting

  • The main shopping street is crowded on weekends
  • Check day-use onsen conditions
  • Morning mist depends on season and weather
  • Confirm travel time between Yufuin and Beppu
Key point

Hours, fees, transport, construction, entry restrictions, and seasonal events can change. Confirm official information shortly before travel.
