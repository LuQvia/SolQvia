Iriomote and Taketomi Island-Hopping Guide

Use Ishigaki as a base to visit Iriomote and Taketomi by ferry, guide, and island transport.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Why visit
  2. Main highlights
  3. Suggested order
  4. Access
  5. Check before visiting

Why visit

The Yaeyama islands differ greatly in environment and transport. Iriomote is large and guide-oriented, while Taketomi centers on village scenery and short local travel; separate days are better than rushing both.

Keep the route focused and confirm the return connection, weather alternative, and primary purpose before departure so transport does not consume the destination time.

Main highlights

  • Iriomote mangroves and forest activities
  • Nature observation with qualified guides
  • Taketomi traditional village and white-sand roads
  • Inter-island ferries from Ishigaki Port

Suggested order

  1. Begin with Iriomote mangroves and forest activities as the main objective.
  2. Continue in the same direction to Nature observation with qualified guides.
  3. If time and energy remain, add Taketomi traditional village and white-sand roads or Inter-island ferries from Ishigaki Port.
  4. Preserve buffer time for meals, rest, luggage, and the return journey.

Access

Use ferries from Ishigaki Port Remote Island Terminal. Iriomote has multiple ports, so choose the one matching the tour, accommodation, and bus route.

Check before visiting

  • Check ferry cancellations, waves, and wind
  • Use qualified guides and review safety on Iriomote
  • Do not enter private property in Taketomi village
  • Reduce impact on nature, water, and waste systems
Key point

Hours, prices, transport, entry rules, construction, natural conditions, and seasonal events change. Recheck official facility, local-government, and transport sources shortly before departure.

Frequently asked questions

How much time should I allow?

Taketomi can be a day trip, but Iriomote benefits from an overnight stay; allow at least three days for both.

Can I visit by public transport?

Use ferries from Ishigaki Port Remote Island Terminal. Iriomote has multiple ports, so choose the one matching the tour, accommodation, and bus route.

What should I verify before departure?

Check current hours, transport schedules, weather, and entry restrictions. For mountains, islands, coasts, and onsen areas, also review closures, cancellations, and natural conditions.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
