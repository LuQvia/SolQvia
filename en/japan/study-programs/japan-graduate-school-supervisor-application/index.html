The central decision is matching research field and supervisor while distinguishing degree and research-student routes. Rules vary by nationality, school, program, status, year, and employer, so recheck official information immediately before applying or signing.

Items to verify

  • Master's, doctoral, and professional degrees
  • Research proposal
  • Contacting a supervisor
  • English programs and admission terms
Key point

Save guidelines, official URLs, application numbers, payment records, submitted documents, deadlines, contacts, and permission details for offline access.

Preparation and application workflow

  1. Define the goal, degree, and teaching language
  2. Compare accreditation, admission, cost, and refund terms
  3. Place documents and tests on a deadline calendar
  4. After admission, complete COE, housing, and travel

Final pre-action review

  • Eligible status, school, or program
  • Application, exam, and contract deadlines
  • Cost, payment, and refund
  • Housing, insurance, and emergency contacts

Prioritize official and written conditions

  • Cross-check school and authority information
  • Save written terms rather than relying on verbal explanations
  • Keep actual activities within the residence status
  • Do not depend only on scholarships or part-time income
  • Prepare alternatives for changes, denial, or withdrawal
Key point

This guide supports general planning. Current rules from the school, Immigration Services Agency, embassy or consulate, testing body, and employer take priority.
