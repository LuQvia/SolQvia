Ohara Rural Kyoto Temple Guide

Plan Sanzenin, Jakko-in, and rural paths around bus schedules and the season.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Why visit
  2. Main highlights
  3. Suggested order

Why visit

Ohara is a rural mountain settlement in northern Kyoto with temple gardens, fields, and village scenery. Bus travel from central Kyoto makes closing times and the return service essential.

Keep the route focused and confirm the return connection first so that transport does not consume the time intended for the destination.

Main highlights

  • Sanzenin gardens and moss
  • Hosenin and Shorinin area
  • Rural path toward Jakko-in
  • Ohara fields and local food

Suggested order

  1. Begin with Sanzenin gardens and moss.
  2. Continue in the same direction to Hosenin and Shorinin area.
  3. If time remains, add Rural path toward Jakko-in or Ohara fields and local food.
  4. Preserve buffer time for food, rest, and the return journey.

Access

Use local buses from Kyoto Station, central Kyoto, or Kokusaikaikan. Expect congestion and crowded vehicles in peak seasons.

Check before visiting

  • Check each temple closing time
  • Aim for a bus before the final service
  • Expect road congestion in autumn
  • Keep noise low within the village
Key point

Hours, prices, transport, entry rules, construction, and seasonal events change. Recheck official facility, local-government, and transport sources shortly before departure.

Frequently asked questions

How much time should I allow?

Allow half a day for the main temples or a full day with Jakko-in and a meal.

Can I visit by public transport?

Use local buses from Kyoto Station, central Kyoto, or Kokusaikaikan. Expect congestion and crowded vehicles in peak seasons.

What should I verify before departure?

Check current hours, transport schedules, weather, and entry restrictions. For mountain, coastal, and onsen areas, also review service suspensions and road closures.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
