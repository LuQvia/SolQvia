Items to verify

  • Schedule and filing deadlines

Key point

Operational workflow

  1. Work backward from move-out and move-in dates
  2. List quotes and administrative procedures
  3. Document before and after transport
  4. Update address, contracts, and mail

Final pre-action review

  • Contract party and contacts
  • Total and extra costs
  • Start, renewal, and cancellation deadlines
  • Photos, itemized bills, and application receipts

Prioritize official terms and evidence

  • Reconcile advertising with the contract
  • Do not rely only on verbal explanations
  • Read special and cancellation clauses
  • Keep dated photos
  • Use Consumer Hotline 188 or another official channel when needed

Frequently asked questions

What should I check first for Planning a Move During Japan's March-April Peak Season?

Start by working backward from candidate dates, time slots, key handover, inspection, and utility start. Save current written information from the contract party, manager, municipality, and provider.

Should I choose a mover based only on price?

No. Compare inventory, work scope, extra charges, liability, cancellation, and packing materials on the same basis.

What records should I keep?

Keep advertisements, quotes, explanations, leases, special clauses, photos, application numbers, bills, cancellation and return receipts, and communications.
