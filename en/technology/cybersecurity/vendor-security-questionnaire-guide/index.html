SaaS Vendor Security Questionnaire: What to Verify Before Contracting

Verify data handling, authentication, encryption, logs, subprocessors, incident notice, and deletion before contracting.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Purpose of this control
  2. Management elements to prioritize
  3. Implementation sequence
  4. What to verify
  5. Ongoing operations checklist

Purpose of this control

Verify data handling, authentication, encryption, logs, subprocessors, incident notice, and deletion before contracting.

Do not rely on certifications alone; verify controls and contractual responsibilities relevant to your use.

Management elements to prioritize

  • Stored data and processing regions
  • SSO, MFA, SCIM, and audit logs
  • Encryption and key management
  • Subprocessors and change notice
  • Incident notification and deletion at termination
Key point

Evaluate ownership, covered assets, evidence, exceptions, and recovery rather than product names alone.

Implementation sequence

  1. Classify intended data
  2. Send a standard questionnaire
  3. Verify evidence and validity dates
  4. Put material gaps into contractual terms
  5. Schedule annual reassessment and change monitoring

What to verify

  • Whether capability is distinguished from current implementation
  • Whether audit logs are included in the chosen plan
  • Whether backup deletion periods are known
  • Whether exit and migration have been tested

Ongoing operations checklist

  • Named an owner and backup owner
  • Can update the asset and data inventory
  • Retain logs and change history
  • Tested stop, isolation, and recovery procedures
  • Scheduled monthly or quarterly review
Key point

Security controls do not guarantee that incidents will never occur. Design layered defenses to reduce impact and accelerate detection and recovery.

Frequently asked questions

Does a small organization need SaaS Vendor Security Questionnaire?

Decide by data sensitivity, outage impact, and exposure rather than company size. Start with Stored data and processing regions, assign an owner, and set a review cycle.

Which baseline metrics should be recorded first?

Record volume, labor, failure rate, recovery time, and cost before implementation. Use the same definitions after rollout to measure benefit and side effects.

Is this a one-time setup?

No. Permissions, pricing, product behavior, threats, regulations, and team size change, so monthly or quarterly review is required.
