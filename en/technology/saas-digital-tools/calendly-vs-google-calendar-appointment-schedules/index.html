Decision summary

Compare Calendly and Google Calendar appointment schedules by booking pages, team coordination, payments, notifications, integrations, and cost.

Choose based on whether you need simple Google-centered booking or richer routing, team scheduling, and external integrations.

Compare under equal conditions

  • Individual, team, round-robin, and group scheduling
  • Intake questions, booking rules, availability, and buffers
  • Payments, SMS, workflows, and routing
  • Non-Google calendars, CRM, and meeting integrations
  • Incremental cost including existing Workspace subscriptions

When Calendly fits better

  • Automatic distribution across sales, recruiting, or support teams
  • Broad CRM and automation integrations
  • Detailed notifications, workflows, and analytics
  • A dedicated external scheduling service

When Google Calendar予約スケジュール fits better

  • Individual or small-team booking inside Google Workspace
  • Publishing booking pages without another major product
  • Operating directly from Google Calendar availability
  • No need for advanced routing or extensive integrations

Final pilot sequence

  1. Price both options for the same users and contract term
  2. Reproduce the same workflow and dataset
  3. Test administration, sharing, audit, and offboarding
  4. Run real import and export tests
  5. Choose based on three-year cost and migration difficulty
Key point

Pricing and features change frequently. Verify official pricing, terms, security documentation, and the trial environment before signing.
