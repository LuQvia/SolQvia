Shopify vs Square Online for Ecommerce and Retail

Compare Shopify and Square Online by ecommerce, store POS, inventory, payments, apps, international selling, pricing, and migration.

In this guide
  1. Decision summary
  2. Compare under equal conditions
  3. When Shopify fits better
  4. When Square Online fits better

Decision summary

Compare Shopify and Square Online by ecommerce, store POS, inventory, payments, apps, international selling, pricing, and migration.

Choose between an ecommerce-first platform built to scale and adding online selling around Square payments and physical retail.

Compare under equal conditions

  • Products, variants, inventory, and locations
  • Themes, content, checkout, and custom domains
  • Store POS, terminals, returns, and customer data
  • Apps, shipping, tax, currencies, and sales channels
  • Subscription, payment, app, theme, and migration cost

When Shopify fits better

  • Scaling ecommerce products, channels, and international sales
  • Using a broad app and theme ecosystem
  • Unifying online and store operations around Shopify
  • Investing in dedicated ecommerce operations

When Square Online fits better

  • Already using Square payments or POS
  • A small retailer adding online sales
  • Prioritizing simple operations over extensive ecommerce customization
  • Focusing on integrated store and payment management

Final pilot sequence

  1. Price both options for the same users and contract term
  2. Reproduce the same workflow and dataset
  3. Test administration, sharing, audit, and offboarding
  4. Run real import and export tests
  5. Choose based on three-year cost and migration difficulty
Key point

Pricing and features change frequently. Verify official pricing, terms, security documentation, and the trial environment before signing.

Frequently asked questions

Can Shopify versus Square Online be tested for free?

Free tiers and trials change by product and date. Check the official pricing page for included features, limits, and what happens after the trial.

What should be verified before adoption?

Define the target process, users, data, approval responsibility, total cost, and export path before comparing feature lists.

How long should a small pilot run?

A two-to-four-week pilot on one representative workflow is usually enough to measure quality, labor, exceptions, and adoption.
