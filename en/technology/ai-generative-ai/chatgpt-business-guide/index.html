ChatGPT Business Guide: Administration, Access, and Data Controls

A practical adoption guide to ChatGPT Business, formerly Team, covering workspace administration, access, billing, and data controls.

Published: 2026-07-22Last updated: 2026-07-22
In this guide
  1. Overview
  2. Core capabilities
  3. Getting started
  4. Practical use cases
  5. Risks and checks

Overview

ChatGPT Business is a multi-user workspace plan with centralized billing, administration, and business data protections. It is the current name for the plan formerly known as ChatGPT Team.

For adoption, define the target workflow, human review responsibility, and data rules before focusing on feature volume.

Core capabilities

  • A workspace designed for teams of two or more
  • Administration for users, billing, authentication, and usage
  • Business data is not used for model training by default
  • Projects, sharing, and connected tools subject to workspace configuration
Key point

Feature names and usage limits change. This guide emphasizes durable selection criteria and operating practices.

Getting started

  1. Define the approved workflows and participating teams.
  2. Assign workspace, billing, and policy owners.
  3. Document prohibited data, sharing, connectors, and offboarding rules.
  4. Pilot with a small group and measure quality and review effort.
  5. Train users and run periodic governance reviews.

Practical use cases

Treat outputs as drafts, options, structured notes, or comparison support rather than automatically approved final work.

  • separating business AI usage from personal subscriptions
  • centralizing user and billing administration
  • governing shared Projects and company knowledge workflows
  • controlling access during employee changes and offboarding

Risks and checks

  • Joining a workspace does not automatically expose every private chat to other members
  • Shared links and connected-source permissions require separate controls
  • Administration alone does not prevent incorrect inputs or decisions
  • Confirm current seat and additional-credit terms at purchase
Key point

High-impact legal, medical, financial, contractual, and safety decisions require primary sources or qualified professional review.

Frequently asked questions

What should I try first?

Start with a small, reviewable task such as “separating business AI usage from personal subscriptions” so a person can verify the result.

Can I use it for free?

Free access and trial conditions can change, so check the official pricing page when you sign up.

Can I enter confidential information?

Do not paste personal, customer, or unpublished information without checking the plan’s data terms and your organization’s policy. Anonymize data when possible.

This article provides general information. Check official sources for current specifications, prices, rules, and opening hours.
