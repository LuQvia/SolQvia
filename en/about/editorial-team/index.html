Editorial team

How SolQvia assigns publishing responsibility, source review, and updates.

Publishing responsibility

The SolQvia Editorial Team is responsible for published content. We do not label an article as expert-reviewed or device-tested unless that work was actually completed.

Workflow

  1. Define the search intent and safety impact.
  2. Review primary sources from OS providers, manufacturers, and carriers.
  3. Order steps by risk and disclose data impact.
  4. Test links, structured data, and language pairing.
  5. Update after specification changes or corrections.

Byline

Articles use the byline “SolQvia Editorial Team.” Each smartphone article separately states its verification level.
